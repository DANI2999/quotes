Earth Quotes

About Earth Quotes

Earth Quotes is a project made for freeCodeCamp. The quotes featured are all about sustainability and conservation to protect the Earth and it's amazing natural landscapes, as well as just travelling, exploring, and enjoying the planet we call home. The images featured are from Google's Earth Views project, which is a collection of some of the most beautiful Google Earth images.
